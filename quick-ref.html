GRIMDARK FUTURE - QUICK REFERENCE

ACTIONS & MOVEMENT

Action Move Notes
Hold 0" Can shoot
Advance 6" Can shoot after moving
Rush 12" Can't shoot
Charge 12" Moves into melee

UNIT COHERENCY

  • Models within 1" of at least one other model
  • Models within 9" of all other models in unit
  • Units only within 1" of other units when charging

QUALITY TESTS

  • Roll ≥ Quality value to succeed
  • 6 always succeeds, 1 always fails

LINE OF SIGHT

  • Models see 360°, can't see through obstacles/units
  • Can see through models from own unit

MISSION & OBJECTIVES

  • Place D3+2 objectives outside deployment zones, >9" apart
  • Unit within 3" of objective with no enemies = seized
  • Most objectives after 4 rounds = winner

UNIVERSAL STRATAGEMS

1CP Command Re-Roll

Re-roll any single die roll

1CP Counter-Attack

Unit strikes first when charged

1CP Heroic Intervention

Move hero up to 6" to join melee

2CP Desperate Effort

+2" move or +1 attack in melee

2CP Get Back Up

On 4+, shaken unit becomes active

1CP Inspiring Presence

Auto-pass morale test

2CP Go To Ground

+2 Defense against shooting

1CP Deadly Strike

One failed block = D3 wounds

COMBAT

SHOOTING

  1. Attacks: Sum Attack value
  2. Hit: Roll ≥ Quality
  3. Block: Roll ≥ Defense
  4. Remove: One model per wound
  • +1 Defense in cover
  • Max 2 targets with different weapon types

MELEE

  1. Chargers move into base contact
  2. Defenders move up to 3" into contact
  3. Strike if within 2" horiz & 4" vert
  4. Hit: Roll ≥ Quality
  5. Block: Roll ≥ Defense
  6. Defenders strike back (optional)

MELEE RESOLUTION

  • Compare wounds: most = winner
  • Loser takes morale test
  • If one unit destroyed, winner may move 3"
  • Otherwise, chargers move back 1"
  • Fatigue: After charging/striking, only hit on 6s in melee until end of round

MORALE

MORALE TRIGGERS

  • Unit at half or less of starting size/tough
  • Unit loses a melee

MORALE TEST RESULTS

  • Pass: No effect
  • Fail: Shaken
  • Fail at half strength in melee: Rout

SHAKEN UNITS

  • Must stay idle
  • Count as fatigued
  • Always fail morale tests
  • Can't contest/seize objectives
  • Spend one activation idle to recover

CAMPAIGN ELEMENTS

EARNING XP & LEVELING

  • +1 XP: Survived battle
  • +1 XP: Killed a unit
  • +2 XP: Killed a hero
  • +3 XP: Killed hero & unit
  • Level up at 5 XP: Regular +25pts, Hero +55pts

CASUALTIES

  • Roll 1D6 per casualty:
  • 1: Dead/Injured
  • 2-5: Recovered
  • 6: Natural Talent (+1 XP)

TERRAIN

Cover: +1 Defense vs shooting if majority inside/behind
Difficult: Max 6" movement through
Dangerous: Roll Tough dice when moving/activating - 1s = wounds
Blocking: Blocks line of sight
Impassable: Can't move through
Elevated: >3" = impassable; ≤3" = can climb

ADVANCED RULES

RANDOM EVENTS

  • Start of each round: Roll 1D6
  • On 5+: Random event happens
  • Roll 2D6 (read as tens & units) to determine event

CASTING SPELLS

  • Caster gets X tokens per round (max 6)
  • Spend tokens = to spell value to cast
  • Roll 4+ to successfully cast
  • Models within 18" can spend tokens to add/subtract from roll

MISSIONS & DEPLOYMENT

  • Special Objectives: +75 points for completing
  • Underdog Bonus: +1 point per 50pts difference. Each point modifies one roll by ±1
  • Scout: After deployment, move up to 12"

TWISTS & GAMBITS

TWISTS

  • Before mission: Roll on Twist table
  • Changes affect entire game

GAMBITS

  • 1/round: Play a Gambit card
  • Effects apply to current activation
  • Can't play multiple with same name

COMMAND POINTS

  • Start with D3+3 CP
  • Gain 1 CP at start of each round
  • Max 12 CP at any time
  • Spend CP on stratagems or abilities
