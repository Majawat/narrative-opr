Melee

Charging models move into base contact or as close as possible. Defenders move up to 3" to do the same. Models within 2" horizontally and 4" vertically must strike with all melee weapons. Defenders may strike back.

Fatigue: After charging or striking back, units only hit on unmodified rolls of 6 in melee until the end of the round.

Morale

At the end of an activation where wounds leave a unit with half or less of its total size/tough value, or it loses a melee, take a quality test. If failed, the unit is Shaken.

Shaken: Must stay idle, counts as fatigued, always fails morale tests, can't contest objectives. Must spend one full activation idle to recover.

Melee Results: Compare wounds caused. The losing unit takes a morale test. If failed while at half strength or less, it routs (removed from play), otherwise Shaken.

Terrain

Cover: Units mostly inside/behind cover get +1 to Defense when blocking hits from shooting.

Difficult: Units moving through difficult terrain can't move more than 6" at a time.

Dangerous: Models moving across or activating in dangerous terrain roll one die (or their tough value). For each roll of 1, the unit takes a wound.

Universal Doctrine
1 CP High Command: +1 to any single die
2 CP Supreme Command: +1 to all dice in roll
2 CP Seize Initiative: Activate instead of enemy
2 CP Waive Initiative: Force opponent to activate
2 CP Delayed Deployment: Pass deployment turn
3 CP Hidden Deployment: Redeploy up to 3 units

Special Rules Reference

AP(X): Targets get -X to Defense rolls when blocking hits. Makes armor less effective.
Ambush: May be set aside before deployment. Deploy anywhere over 9" from enemies after round 1. Cannot seize/contest objectives on deployment round.
Blast(X): Ignores cover. Each hit multiplied by X (max = models in target unit). Devastating against large units.
Caster(X): Gets X spell tokens each round (max 6). Spend tokens equal to spell value to cast. Roll 4+ to succeed. Nearby units can spend tokens for +1/-1 to rolls.
Counter: Strikes first when charged. Charging unit gets -1 Impact roll per Counter model. Good defensive weapon.
Deadly(X): Multiply wound by X. Resolved first; wounds don't carry over. Effective against tough models.
Entrenched: -2 to be hit when shot at from >9" if hasn't moved since last activation. Rewards defensive positioning.
Fast: +2" on Advance, +4" on Rush/Charge. Significantly boosts mobility.
Fear(X): Counts as dealing +X wounds when determining melee winner. Makes enemies more likely to fail morale.
Fearless: On failed morale test, roll one die. On 4+ it counts as passed instead. Greatly improves reliability.
Flying: Move through units and terrain, ignoring terrain effects while moving. Exceptional tactical mobility.
Furious: When charging, 6s to hit in melee deal one extra hit. Increases damage on charges.
Hero: May join one multi-model unit (without another Hero). Can take morale tests for unit. Uses unit's Defense until others are killed.
Immobile: May only use Hold actions, cannot move under any circumstances.
Impact(X): Roll X dice when charging (not if fatigued). Each 2+ = one hit. Adds significant charge damage.
Indirect: -1 to hit when shooting after moving. Can target without line of sight. Ignores cover from obstructions. Good for artillery.
Lance: When charging, +1 to hit and AP(+1) in melee. Makes charges deadlier.
Lock-On: Ignores cover and all negative modifiers to hit and range. Perfect against hidden enemies.
Limited: Only usable once per game. Typically for powerful weapons/abilities.
Poison: Ignores Regeneration. Target must re-roll Defense rolls of 6. Counters regenerating foes.
Regeneration: When taking a wound, roll a die. On 5+ it's ignored. Provides significant durability.
Relentless: When using Hold and shooting, 6s to hit deal one extra hit. Rewards stationary firing.
Reliable: Attacks at Quality 2+, regardless of normal Quality. Greatly increases hit chance.
Rending: Ignores Regeneration. Unmodified 6s to hit get AP(4). Occasional high-penetration hits.
Scout: After all deployment, place anywhere within 12" of position. Great for early positioning.
Slow: -2" on Advance, -4" on Rush/Charge. Significant mobility penalty.
Sniper: Shoots at Quality 2+. Each model can target any model in the unit. Resolved before other weapons.
Stealth: Enemies get -1 to hit when shooting from >9" away. Better survival at range.
Strider: Ignores difficult terrain effects when moving. Maintains mobility everywhere.
Tough(X): Takes X wounds to kill. Continue putting wounds on tough model with most wounds until killed.
Transport(X): Carries up to X models. Heroes with Tough(6) count as 1, non-heroes with Tough(3) count as 3 each.

Mission & Campaign

Mission 2: Signal in the Storm

Overview: Forces must navigate electrical storms that disable technology while racing to extract a component from a deteriorating facility.

Primary Objective: Component Extraction - Gather access codes from at least two security terminals before entering the chamber, then extract the component.

Special Rules:
  • Terminals (9 markers): Roll D3 when reached:
    • 1: Storm Control - Shield terrain, grant cover.
    • 2: Data Terminal - Gain 1 XP.
    • 3: Security Terminal - Get coded data. Two pieces unlock chamber.
  • Electrical Storm: Roll D6 each round. On 4+, storm active:
    • Mechanical/Robot units: -1 to hit
    • Energy weapons: range halved
    • Sheltered units ignore penalty
    • Jump/Flyer/Fast units: dangerous terrain tests

Victory: Secure component and extract it off the board.

Campaign Rules
Random Events: Roll D6 at start of each round, on 5+ a random event occurs.
Mission Length: If no winner after round 4, play continues until there is a winner.
Mission Goals: Winner gets 2 VP and 150pts. Losers get 300pts.
Casualties: Units fully destroyed/routed are casualties. Roll D6 for each:
  • 1: Dead - Remove from army
  • 2-5: Recovered - No penalties
  • 6: Natural Talent - +1 XP
XP Earned:
  • +1 XP: Not a casualty
  • +1 XP: Killed a unit
  • +2 XP: Killed a hero unit
  • +3 XP: Killed a hero and its unit
Unit Leveling: At 5 XP, unit costs +25pts and gains a trait (roll D6).
Hero Leveling: At 5 XP, hero costs +55pts and gains a skill set, then roll for trait.
